Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a popular time management strategy that employs a structured approach to boost productivity and maintain focus during work or study sessions. It involves breaking tasks into manageable intervals, typically 25 minutes each, known as "pomodoros." During each pomodoro, you concentrate solely on the task at hand, eliminating distractions and interruptions. After completing a pomodoro, you take a short break of around 5 minutes to recharge and relax.

Todo List

A todo list is a practical tool for organizing tasks, helping you stay on track and prioritize efficiently. It enables you to jot down all your tasks and responsibilities, making it easier to manage your time and ensure nothing is overlooked. By having a clear overview of your tasks, you can achieve a sense of accomplishment as you check them off. Todo lists are a valuable aid in maintaining productivity and reducing the feeling of overwhelm that can arise from an unstructured workload.
